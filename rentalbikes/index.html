Rental Bikes

We offer rental bikes for our visitors and give them the opportunity to experience the Icelandic landscape on high end trail bikes.

We offer you the chance to rent a high end trail bike from Commencal. This bike is capable going both up and down the mountains and is the perfect weapon to take on the challenges of the trip.

Commencal Meta TR

The Commencal Meta TR offers 140mm rear travel and a 160mm front travel. Combined with 29" wheels this bike is as capable going up and down hills. This is the perfect bike for you to get the best experience possible from our trip.

Setting up the rental bikes

Our goal is to provide you with the best possible experience. That is why we setup each rental bike to your need, based on your weight and riding style.

Maintenance

Our bikes receive a tune-up after each ride. We ensure that all components are in good conditions to give you the best experiance and to keep you safe.
