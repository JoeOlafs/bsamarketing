Mountain Bike Tours

Explore the unique landscape Iceland offers in a guided mountian bike tour

Our Tours

From Snow to Sea

We offer tours that gives you the opportunity to ride trails that span from the roots of Sólheimajökull glacier down to the Atlantic ocean. Join us on a trip to explore the canyons, rivers and valleys that cover the landscape.

Half Day

During this three hour tour you get to experience the nature on a mixture of flow and technical trails. You get to see views that come second to none and experience a different side of Iceland.
Two available starting times.

Full Day

During this six hour tour you get to experience the Icelandic nature by riding a mizture of flow and technical trails. You get to see views that come second to none and experience a different side of Iceland.
During this trip there will be a lunchbreak at base camp.

Coaching

If you are new to mountain biking or feel as you need to work on particular skills you can join us on one day mountain biking coures. We offer sessions for every skill level, simply select skill level during the booking process.
During this training session you get to experience the views and trail network we have to offer at Black Sand Adventures.
